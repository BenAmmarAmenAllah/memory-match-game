# Audio Assets

Please place your audio files in this directory:

- `background-music.mp3` - Looping background music
- `flip.mp3` - Card flip sound
- `match.mp3` - Pair matched sound
- `win.mp3` - Game won sound
- `lose.mp3` - Game lost sound
- `hint.mp3` - Hint used sound

Ensure they are in MP3 format or update `src/context/AudioContext.jsx` to match your file types.
